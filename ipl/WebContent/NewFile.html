Now to give a brief on how the game works. It is a touch different than the usual Fantasy Leagues.

Concept:

 

Let say 4 Members have registered (Yogi, VJ, Shanks and Subhash).

1.       Today’s match is Kolkata Knight Riders  vs Royal challengers Bangalore.

2.       For each match all 4 Members will select best 7 players from the today’s match playing teams(KKR and RCB) . Once player selection time is closed system will randomly decide who’s 5 players team squad is going to compete against another members selected 5 players squad. You may know of your opponent once the voting is closed and Match begins.

·         Yogi’s 7 players squad vs Shanks 7 players squad

 

·         Subhash’s  7 players squad vs VJ’s 7 player squad.

3.       Once match is finished, System will calculate points for your selected 7 players.

 

Calculation of points will be as follow:

·         2 runs means 1 point.

·         1 Wicket means 10 points.

 

4.       On base of calculation, system will decide the winner and credit/debit the points into your account. Let say in Yogi and Shanks, Yogi’s players scored 150 points and Shanks players scored 120 points, now difference is 30 points, so yogi’s account will be credited by 30 points and Shanks account will be debited by 30 points.

 

5.       On next match, again system will randomly decide which member is going to compete against which members.

 

Rules:

 

1.       Submission will be allowed till match starts. That is 4PM for day matches and 8PM for Night Matches.

 

2.       Vote/Player Selection via Message, WhatsApp, eMail, etc..  is not available. J

 

3.       Registration fee is Rs.50/-

 

4.       If in case you fail to select your players before the match, System will perform a random selection to represent you on the day. System though will not take ensure that player is in the final 11 representing the teams.
